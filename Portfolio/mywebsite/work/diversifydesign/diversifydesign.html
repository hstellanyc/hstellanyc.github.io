    Diversify Design

    Book

    As graphic design becomes more globalized, the future of design will engage "outside the narrow critical confines of its existing canon." (Harsh Patel)

    This book is a collection of written works by designers who speak on the topic of underrepresentation and cultural diversity, aiming to push the current design boundaries to recognize a wider range of identities and world views.
